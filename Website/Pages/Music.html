Music

In 2015, Cavetown began posting original songs and covers on his YouTube channel, which quickly gained attention and a dedicated fan base. Some of his most popular tracks include "This Is Home," "Lemon Boy," and "Boys Will Be Bugs." He has released several albums and EPs, including "Cavetown" (2018), "Sleepyhead" (2020), and "Man's Best Friend" (2021).

Cavetown has a diverse discography with a wide range of songs that cover various themes and emotions. His songs often delve into personal experiences, relationships, introspection, and mental health. Here are some notable songs by Cavetown:#

  • "This Is Home": Released in 2015, this song quickly became one of Cavetown's most popular tracks. It's an introspective and heartfelt song that explores feelings of belonging, comfort, and finding a place to call home.
  • "Lemon Boy": Released in 2018, "Lemon Boy" is another highly acclaimed song by Cavetown. It tells the story of a character named Lemon Boy who is an embodiment of self-acceptance and embracing one's uniqueness. The song carries a positive message about staying true to oneself.
  • "Boys Will Be Bugs": This song, released in 2018, resonated with many listeners, particularly teenagers. It explores the challenges and complexities of growing up, navigating relationships, and dealing with emotions.
  • "Devil Town": Originally released by Daniel Johnston, Cavetown's cover of "Devil Town" gained popularity. It captures a sense of longing and the haunting feeling of being trapped in a place that holds negative associations.
